Fun facts about Opticians!

A US Navy optician adjusting a customer's glasses

An Optician does the following (not a complete list):

  • Interprets and fills spectacle and contact lens prescriptions written by the eye doctor
  • Assists the customer in appropriate frame selection
  • Takes all measurements needed for the lab to manufacture the lenses
  • Adjusts & repairs frames, ensuring a perfect fit
  • Inspects eyewear, making sure it passes quality guidelines
  • Keeps inventory, tracks sales and sees to day to day optical shop duties

An Optician is NOT a:

  1. Medical Doctor (Ophthalmologist)
  2. Doctor of Optometry (Optometrist)
  3. Deliverer of Babies (Obstetrician)
  4. Licensed Therapist to whom who you tell all your troubles

The 3 O's:

Profession Abbreviation Years of training
Ophthalmologist MD 12
Optometrist OD 8
Optician RDO 2-3
